FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

When do I pay for my order?

Full payment is required at checkout for regular items. Custom orders require a 50% non-refundable deposit to confirm the order.

Is there an age requirement to place an order?

You must be 18 years or older to place an order. Customers under 18 years old must have parental consent to complete the purchase.

What if I don't likr the taste of my order?

Taste is subjective. We do not offer refunds based on personal taste preferences. We recommend sample testing before purchase.
